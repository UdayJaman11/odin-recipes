Rice

Description

Rice is one of the most important dishes in the Indian sub-continent. We eat rice three times a day. That's why it is important to learn how to cook rice.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Rice cooker
  • Water
  • Electricity

Steps

  1. Wash the rice
  2. Put it in a rice cooker
  3. Put water in it. Use 2 to 1 water to rice ratio.
