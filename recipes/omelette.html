Omelette (Fried egg)

Description

It is an easy-to-cook but delicious meal that has helped students survive in the difficult condition at the hostel. Let's learn how to make an omelette!

Ingredients

  • An egg
  • Salt
  • Soybean oil
  • Onion
  • Chili

Steps

  1. Break the egg's outer area using some hard objects and put it in a bowl or cup
  2. Cut the onions and chilies
  3. Put the onions and chilies in the bowl and mix them with a spoon
  4. Take some soybean oil on a wok and heat it up (DO NOT put the egg now)
  5. After some heating the oil, now you can put the egg created in step three on the wok
  6. Turn the omelette upside down
  7. After some proper cooking, you are ready to bring the omelette in a plate and eat!
