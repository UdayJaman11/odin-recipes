Salad

Description

Salad is a healthy food that one can take with regular meals. It can also be eaten separately.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Cucumber
  • Carrot
  • Onion
  • Pepper
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Wash the ingredients with Water
  2. Cut all the ingredients accordingly
  3. Take them in a bowl
  4. Add some salt and mix all the ingredients with a spoon
  5. Ready!
